Circuitry

Circuitry is a 2D top-down twin-stick roguelike shooter heavily inspired by 2015 indie hit Nuclear Throne. The player is a robot with the ability to dash and wield guns. Guide them through an infinite journey as they make their way through dangerous abandoned wastelands, run down by hostile and armed drones. A project completed over 3 months by around 25 University of Michigan students.

DESOLATION PLACE

Desolation Place is a 3D first-person stealth horror game where the player character must traverse through an abandoned castle, avoiding both the horrors that lurk within its shadows as well as the terrors that lurk within his past. A long term project completed over 3.5 months by around 25 University of Michigan students.
