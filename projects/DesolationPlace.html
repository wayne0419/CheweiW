ABOUT

Desolation Place is a 3D first-person stealth horror game where the player character must traverse through an abandoned castle, avoiding both the horrors that lurk within its shadows as well as the terrors that lurk within his past. A long term project completed over 3.5 months by around 25 University of Michigan students.

DEVELOPMENT INFO

  • Developed by WolverineSoft Studio
  • 2 month development cycle (06/2020 - 08/2020)
  • 25 developers
  • Made using Unity Engine
