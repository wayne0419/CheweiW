ABOUT

Circuitry is a 2D top-down twin-stick roguelike shooter heavily inspired by 2015 indie hit Nuclear Throne. The player is a robot with the ability to dash and wield guns. Guide them through an infinite journey as they make their way through dangerous abandoned wastelands, run down by hostile and armed drones. A long term project completed over 3 months by around 25 University of Michigan students.

DEVELOPMENT INFO

  • Developed by WolverineSoft Studio
  • 2 month development cycle (09/2020 - 12/2020)
  • 25 developers
  • Made using Unity Engine
