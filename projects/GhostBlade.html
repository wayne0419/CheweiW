Ghost Blade

ABOUT

Ghost Blade is a VR hack-and-slash game featuring ability to deflect enemies shurikens with a katana.

DEVELOPMENT INFO

  • 1.5 month development cycle (10/2022 - 12/2022)
  • 6 developers
  • Made using Unity Engine

CONTRIBUTIONS

  • Programming, Art, Audio, Design

THOUGHTS

Ghost Blade is my first attempt on developing a VR game.
VR HMD & controllers provide control experience that is very unique from traditional mouse & keyboard or gamepad setup.
I experimented with some of them and finally decided to work around a gameplay mechanic that feels most satisfying and provides much better experience in VR setup than in traditional setup : Cutting & Deflecting Bullets.

To summarize the feeling of this gameplay mechanic, it is like Beat Saber or Fruit Ninja, but with enemies.
